Problems

Here are some of the problems I came up with, specifically in a COVD world.

Mask Convenience

Mask Convenience

Masks aren't always convenient- wearing them for long periods of time, having your hands full when trying to put on your mask, or simply remembering your mask in general have been reccurring problems.

School Stress

School Stress

Classes at UChicago are hard, and they can seem even harder when classes are online, and potentially asynchronous. This quarter has induced much more anxiety because now, students have to worry about their mental and personal health on top of school work.

Personal Health

Personal Health

COVID has introduced many important thoughts and discussions, with one important one being how comforatable we are doing certain things during COVID. Sometimes, it feels like we are put in a position between choosing our personal health and our social interactions with others.

Lack of Community

Lack of Community

With almost everything being virtual, the world has become lonely. As students, we are lacking the sense of community we are used to in college. Specifically, I beleive the lack of communal spaces we once had (library, dining halls, gym, classrooms, etc.) has made it feel more isolating.

Ideas

Based on these problems, I came up with a few start-up ideas shown below.

Snap Mask

Snap Mask is an elastic piece that connects the straps of the mask together, forming a convenient easy way to wear your mask for long peiods of time without hurting your ears. No more fidgeting with your mask, forgetting to put it on when you leave your house, or it getting stuck on your headphones or jewelry.

COVID Community

COVID Community is an app that allows people to connect with others. It is a place for people to meet others, talk with their friends, and create community in a low risk, casual setting. Users can create bubbles, or groups within their communities. You can enter the bubble at any time via text, call, or video to let people know your free and want to hang out.

COVID Tracker

COVID Tracker is an app for people to provide updates on their relationship with COVID. The user would be able to log in, connect with friends, and fill out a personal COVID card. This card would provide information such as if you've had COVID, what degree you're comforatable seeing people, if you are quarantined, etc.
